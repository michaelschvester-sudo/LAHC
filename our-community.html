Our Commitment to Our Community

Rooted in Vancouver

Live At Home Care is a proudly Canadian-owned and operated company, deeply rooted in Vancouver and Greater Vancouver. With 20 years of experience as part of Simpe Q, we’ve built a legacy of trust, serving seniors across our local communities with dedication and care.

Your Neighbors, Your Caregivers

Our team consists of local residents—your neighbors—who are passionate about providing exceptional care. We hire individuals driven to support their community, ensuring every senior receives personalized, compassionate care from people who understand the local way of life.

Not a Franchise, but a Local Partner

Unlike large, franchised home care agencies, Live At Home Care is independently operated, with a deep understanding of Vancouver’s neighborhoods. We’re not just a business; we’re part of the community, committed to serving those who have supported us.

Our Motto: Giving Back

Our motto is simple: serve the community that’s served us. We exist to enhance the lives of seniors in Vancouver and Greater Vancouver, providing care that reflects our gratitude and commitment to the place we call home.
