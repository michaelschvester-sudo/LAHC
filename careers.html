Home Care Aide

Provide compassionate, hands-on care to seniors, helping them maintain independence in their homes.

Responsibilities:

  • Assist with daily activities such as bathing, dressing, and grooming.
  • Support mobility and light housekeeping tasks.
  • Monitor and report changes in clients’ health to care coordinators.
  • Provide companionship and emotional support.

Requirements:

  • Certified Home Care Aide (HCA) or equivalent training.
  • Valid First Aid and CPR certification.
  • Clear criminal background check.
  • Excellent communication and empathy skills.
  • Ability to work flexible hours in Greater Vancouver.

Caregiver

Offer personalized support to seniors, focusing on companionship and daily assistance to enhance their quality of life.

Responsibilities:

  • Provide companionship through social activities and conversation.
  • Assist with errands, meal preparation, and light housekeeping.
  • Support clients with Alzheimer’s or dementia with patience and care.
  • Coordinate with families to ensure client needs are met.

Requirements:

  • Experience in caregiving or related field (certification preferred).
  • Valid driver’s license and reliable transportation.
  • Clear criminal background check.
  • Strong interpersonal skills and compassion.
  • Availability for part-time or full-time shifts.

IT/Tech Specialist

Help seniors stay connected by providing tech support for devices, video calls, and smart home systems.

Responsibilities:

  • Set up and troubleshoot Zoom, smartphones, tablets, and smart home devices.
  • Provide one-on-one tech training tailored to seniors’ needs.
  • Assist with basic IT tasks, such as software updates and device maintenance.
  • Document client tech setups for ongoing support.

Requirements:

  • Experience in IT support or tech training (degree or certification a plus).
  • Patience and ability to explain technical concepts clearly.
  • Clear criminal background check.
  • Valid driver’s license for on-site visits in Greater Vancouver.
  • Flexible schedule, including evenings or weekends.
