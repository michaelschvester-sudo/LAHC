About Live At Home Care

Live At Home Care is a proud division of Simpe Q, a leading provider of senior care services in British Columbia since 2003, now boasting over 20 years of experience serving seniors across Vancouver, Vancouver Island, North Vancouver, and the Lower Mainland. Our dedicated team employs full-time certified Home Care Assistants, Licensed Practical Nurses, and caregivers, all trained and mentored with an exclusive approach to senior caregiving.

Our Values

We pledge to give our best in every service we provide. We guarantee seniors the respect and dignity they deserve, honoring the unique history and character shaped by their life.

Our Team

Experienced Home Care Assistants (HCA), Licensed Practical Nurses (LPN), caregivers, and IT specialists for personalized support.

Our Services

We offer a range of services to support seniors in living independently, from personal care, at-home yoga/fall prevention, and nursing to innovative IT solutions.

Personal Care

Support with bathing, dressing, mobility, and daily tasks for comfort and independence.

Companionship

Social visits, errands, and medical escorts to reduce isolation.

IT/Tech Support

Setup for ZOOM, smart home devices, and tech training to stay connected.

Alzheimer's & Dementia Care

Compassionate support with memory care and daily assistance to enhance quality of life.

Medication Management

Assistance with medication reminders and management.

Nursing Services

Professional care by registered LPNs for wound care, medication, and more.
